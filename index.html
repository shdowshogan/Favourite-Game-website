Game Overview

Grand Theft Auto V (GTA V) is an action-adventure game developed by Rockstar North and published by Rockstar Games. Released in 2013, the game is set in the fictional state of San Andreas, which is based on Southern California. Players can control three protagonists: Michael De Santa, Franklin Clinton, and Trevor Philips, and switch between them to complete missions and explore the open-world environment.

Characters

Franklin

He is the first character that you will encounter while playing the game. As a young and ambitious repo man, Franklin is eager to rise above the limitations of his life in Los Santos. Franklin's journey begins with small-time hustles and car repossessions, but his life takes a dramatic turn when he crosses paths with Michael De Santa, a retired bank robber.

Michael

Michael De Santa is a retired bank robber who now lives a life of luxury in the affluent neighborhood of Rockford Hills. Despite his wealth, Michael is dissatisfied and struggles with a dysfunctional family. His past catches up with him when he meets Franklin, and together, they embark on a series of high-stakes heists. Michael's expertise and connections in the criminal underworld play a crucial role in the unfolding story. His character is marked by a deep desire for redemption and a complicated relationship with his past crimes.

Trevor

Trevor Philips is an unpredictable and violent character whose erratic behavior makes him one of the most dangerous individuals in Los Santos. As a former military pilot and Michael's old partner in crime, Trevor's life is filled with chaos and mayhem. His journey is marked by a relentless pursuit of power and dominance in the criminal underworld. Trevor's volatile nature and unique personality bring a wild and intense dynamic to the story. Despite his brutality, he remains fiercely loyal to those he considers friends, adding depth to his complex character..
